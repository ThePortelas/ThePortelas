Dress Code

What to Wear

Evening Attire
Formal / Black-Tie Optional

Think elevated and chic: suits or tuxes, long dresses or dressy cocktail looks. The celebration is outdoors on estate grounds, so consider block heels or shoes that are grass-friendly.
